What is a sharpgroup?

A sharpgroup is a tool to create a custom pool for you and your bros. We are NOT a gambling website. We also can not stop you from charging all your friends 10 bucks to enter the sharpgroup. We also can't stop you from paying out the leader of your sharpgroup. And we also can't stop you from shaming the guy who doesn't pay his dues on time by drawing boobs on his face when he passes out at the tailgate after-party. BUT AGAIN WE ARE NOT A GABMBLING WEBSITE!

Fantasy Sportsbook

Fantasy Sportsbook is a game that is loosely based on sports betting. You make wagers with fake money AKA sharps. Whoever has the most money at the end of the defined season wins the sharpgroup. This game can be played at any time of the year, for any combinition of available sports currently in season. Sports include NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, PGA Tour, Triple Crown Races, NCAAB and NCAAF
